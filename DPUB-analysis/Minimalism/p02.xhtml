Part 2

Living Lighter, One Room at a Time

IN THIS PART …

  • Prepare to get rid of excess possessions by building a game plan and reviewing decluttering fundamentals that work in any space.
  • Decide what a minimalist wardrobe means to you and review your clothes and accessories to keep what fits the bill.
  • Understand how your sleep is affected by having too much stuff, and declutter your bedroom and your bathroom.
  • Determine how much kitchen storage space you have and pare back kitchen items so you keep only what can fit.
  • Assess and remove unneeded items from your living space, your work space, and your storage space.
  • Become an intentional shopper and find out how to take care of the items you keep.
