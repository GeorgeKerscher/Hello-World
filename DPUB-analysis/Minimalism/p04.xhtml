Part 4

Dialing Down the Digital Overload

IN THIS PART …

  • Assess how dependent you are on screens and digital devices, and take advantage of built-in functionality and mindfulness to keep your device use in check.
  • Organize your digital files, photos, and emails for easy access, and change your mobile device habits to better support your goals.
  • Disentangle yourself from excessive scrolling, passive watching, and virtual gaming so you can spend more time with real-world people and hobbies.
