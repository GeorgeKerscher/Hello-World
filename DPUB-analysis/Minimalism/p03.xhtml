Part 3

Aligning Your Money with What Matters

IN THIS PART …

  • Identify your values and financial goals, and determine your net worth so you can monitor your financial health from day one.
  • Figure out where you spend your income and create a budget that supports your financial values.
  • Understand the different types of debt and get rid of the money you owe.
  • Discover minimalist-friendly investments, and take advantage of technology to make it easier to monitor your money.
